ABOUT US

Nestled in the heart of Festac town Lagos state, Kechmelondeliciousness is a culinary haven dedicated to celebrating the rich tapestry of
African cuisine and unique pastries. Step into our world and let your taste buds embark on a journey of discovery unlike any other.


Vision Statement:

Our aim is to be the premier destination for culinary excellence, setting new standards through innovative creations and unwavering
commitment to quality, creativity and customer satisfaction.


Mission Statement:

Our mission is to create a memorable culinary experience, for our customers, fostering, joy and connection through the art of cooking
and baking while contributing positively to our community and environment .

CORE VALUES:

*Excellence
*Creativity
*Quality
*Customer Satisfaction
*Sustainability
*Community Engagement
*Integrity
